Questions people ask

How are cohorts matched?

We use a diagnostic that maps where you sit on motivation vs. execution, then have a real conversation. We match based on the tensions you're navigating—not your industry, not your title. A Dreamer in fintech has more in common with a Dreamer in healthcare than with the Optimizer down the hall.

What if my organization isn't "ready" for AI?

If your organization is still building the business case for why AI matters, this probably isn't the right moment. But if you're past that—and now navigating implementation, governance, talent, culture—this is exactly who we built this for.

Is this a vendor pitch?

No. We're not selling software. We're not consultants looking for projects. This is peer learning—facilitated by people who've done this work, with access to a network of founders building frontier AI. That's it.

What's the time commitment?

Four gatherings per year (2 in-person, 2 virtual). Monthly community calls. Ongoing Slack access. You engage as much or as little as you want between gatherings—but the value comes from showing up.

Why Strange VC?

We invest in next-generation tech startups. That means we have direct access to founders building at the edge—not what's hyped, but what's actually emerging. That signal is part of what you get.

Can I bring my team?

Yes. We're exploring group rates for up to 3 people from the same organization. Sometimes the leverage is having allies inside who are learning the same things.

What happens after the first year?

Many cohorts continue. Some evolve. We'll figure that out together based on what's valuable.
